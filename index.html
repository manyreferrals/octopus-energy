Octopus provides cheap, green energy

All Octopus tariffs use 100% green electricity — and they're no more expensive than competitors' non-green tariffs. You can also carbon offset the gas you use if you want to minimise your environmental impact even further.

Switching is easy

Octopus have worked hard to make the switching process as quick and painless as it can be. You can sign up in minutes, you will usually be switched over within 17 days and it's covered by the Energy Switch Guarantee. There will be no supply disruption and no need for engineer visits. Octopus will handle everything and give you regular updates on the progress of your switch.

Fantastic customer service

Octopus has been a Which? recommended energy provider for 3 years running and is rated 5 stars on Trustpilot. Octopus answers calls in 2 minutes on average and is also reachable 7 days a week via Email, Twitter and Facebook. Octopus does not charge exit fees to any customers on any tariff, so you are free to leave whenever you want.
